Circumpolar Constellation Overview: North Hemisphere

These are the stars always visible in the Northern Hemisphere, hover over with the mouse to discover their data, stories and myths.
Ursa Minor, Ursa Major, Cassiopeia, Cepheus, Draco and Camelopardus can be seen every night of the year. This is because they are at the height of the north celestial pole, with paths within the boundaries of the North Star, never disappearing below the horizon. They are there, everlasting reliable guides, conquering a place in the humans lives as Mythological creatures, with names and personalities.
